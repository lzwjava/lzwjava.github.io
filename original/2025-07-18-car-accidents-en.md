There are some car accidents in my life. The most severe happend in April 2018, after 1 month I got the driver license. I travled to Xilinhot, Inner Mongolia,.

I droved like 100 km an hour with my wife. After a highway exit which is just a toll and it doesn't have curve road, and it is in the straight road, I thought I am still in the highway. So I didn't break my car a bit. And suddenly there is a crossroad.

My car crashed into a white car who just was turning around his car. And my car was moving like 10 meters away too. Things got messay. His car was moved into the road divider. 

I was fine as I wear the safe belt and my body was complete and without any hurt. My wife's shoulder has some problem. 

However, I can see that ther other car was hit hard. And I went to check the case. I am afraid that I probably kill people.

I went to see that , and found a male driver alone. And his head was full of blood. I called the medical emergecy and police.

Actually, we were lucky for that before hitting his car, there was a big truck at front of mine. We were glad that this big truck drove fast and missed our car. Otherwise, our car probably drove into the down part of the big truck. That probably made our bodies very dangerous.

Later, the insurance companies of my car helped me. I just bought the second-hand car half a month before. So the insurances were bought by the original owner of my car.

This gave a life-long memory to me. And later accidents of mine are actually trivil.

I gald that car accidents in my life now doesn't kill me. So I record here to wish I can have less car accidents in my later life, so hope you are same too.
