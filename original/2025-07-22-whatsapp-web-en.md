I am happy to use WhatsApp Web today. It provided a completely new experience. I first registered for the WhatsApp app in 2019 when I needed to communicate with a software client from Glassdoor. I earned 450 euros for that project.

However, I didn’t use WhatsApp again until 2022, when I was working as a contractor at a Singaporean bank. I noticed my colleagues using the WhatsApp Business app, so I switched to it. Initially, registering an account was not easy. My phone number in China couldn’t receive the SMS verification code, but I managed to resolve the issue later.

I then used the WhatsApp Mac app for some time. However, for some reason, the login QR code wouldn’t display. As a result, I switched to using WhatsApp Web.

It’s an amazing experience. I think it’s much better than the WeChat Web experience. One reason is that I have fewer contacts on WhatsApp, which makes it faster.

I can imagine that a dedicated team has been working on it for years. I’m grateful for their efforts and can truly feel their passion for creating something valuable for the world.
