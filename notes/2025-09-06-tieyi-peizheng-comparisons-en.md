## Guangzhou Tie Yi High School (铁一中)

* **History & Accreditation**
  Founded in 1952 as “Guangzhou Railway No.1 High School.” In 2004, it became a city-level public school. Today, it is a Guangdong Provincial First-Class School and a National Demonstration High School.

* **Campuses**
  It has three campuses: Yuexiu (main campus), Panyu, and Baiyun.

* **Academic Performance**
  The school is renowned for outstanding results in the National College Entrance Exam (Gaokao). Key statistics:

  * Key university admission rate often exceeds 40%.
  * Overall university admission rate is above 90%.
  * 100% college enrollment rate in recent years.
  * Key admission rates have repeatedly surpassed 90%, among the best in Guangzhou.

* **Faculty & Resources**
  Tie Yi has many top-level teachers (special-grade and senior teachers), strong faculty, and serves as a provincial and national teacher training base.

* **School Culture**
  Known for excellent school spirit and a strong academic atmosphere. Parents and students widely recognize its balanced culture and achievements.

## Guangzhou Peizheng Middle School (培正中学)

* **Historical Roots**
  Its history traces back to 1889. After several relocations and renamings, it restored the name “Peizheng Middle School” in 1985.

* **Accreditation**
  Located in Yuexiu District, it is also a Guangdong Provincial First-Class School and a National Demonstration High School.

* **Campus & Architecture**
  Famous for its historic campus. It preserves many heritage buildings, such as the 1934 school gate, library, Meizhou Building, and Australia Building, all of which are culturally significant.

* **Academics & Life**
  Peizheng is well known for its strong academic tradition and holistic approach to education. The school also organizes open-campus days for parents and students to experience its environment.

* **Cultural Legacy**
  Its motto is “Pursue the Highest Good, Uphold Integrity.” Peizheng is recognized for unique traditions such as its “class societies,” its school motto, and heritage-rich teaching style.

### Comparison (without table)

* **History**: Tie Yi is relatively younger (founded 1952) and focuses on modern public education, while Peizheng has deep roots going back to the late 19th century and emphasizes tradition and culture.

* **Academic Focus**: Tie Yi is known for its high university admission rates, rigorous teaching, and strong faculty; Peizheng stands out for its rich cultural atmosphere, historical environment, and holistic development.

* **Location**: Both are located in Guangzhou’s Yuexiu District. Tie Yi also runs additional campuses in Panyu and Baiyun.
