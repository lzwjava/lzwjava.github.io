- **Refrigerator**: Keeps food fresh by maintaining a cool temperature, with separate compartments for freezing and refrigeration.
- **Microwave Oven**: Quickly heats or cooks food using microwave radiation, ideal for reheating leftovers or preparing quick meals.
- **Washing Machine**: Cleans clothes automatically by agitating them in water with detergent, often with customizable cycles.
- **Dishwasher**: Automatically cleans dishes, cutlery, and cookware using hot water and detergent, saving time and effort.
- **Oven/Stove**: Combines a stovetop for cooking in pans and an oven for baking or roasting at controlled temperatures.
- **Toaster**: Browns bread slices or bagels using heated coils, typically with adjustable settings for desired crispness.
- **Blender**: Purees, mixes, or chops food with rotating blades, great for smoothies, sauces, or soups.
- **Coffee Maker**: Brews coffee by dripping hot water through ground coffee, often with programmable or single-serve options.
- **Vacuum Cleaner**: Removes dust and debris from floors and surfaces using suction, with attachments for various cleaning tasks.
- **Air Conditioner**: Cools indoor spaces by circulating refrigerant, often with adjustable temperature and fan settings.
- **Television**: Displays video and audio content for entertainment, news, or gaming, often with smart streaming capabilities.
- **Iron**: Smooths wrinkles from clothes using heat and steam, with adjustable settings for different fabrics.
- **Hair Dryer**: Blows hot or cool air to dry and style hair, typically with multiple speed and heat settings.
- **Electric Kettle**: Boils water quickly using an electric heating element, often with auto-shutoff for safety.
- **Ceiling Fan**: Circulates air to cool or distribute heat in a room, with adjustable speeds and sometimes lighting.
- **Air Purifier**: Removes contaminants like dust, pollen, pet dander, and smoke from indoor air using filters (often HEPA) or other technologies like UV or ionizers, improving air quality for health and comfort.
- **Dehumidifier**: Extracts excess moisture from the air using a cooling coil or desiccant, preventing mold, mildew, and dampness, especially in humid climates or basements.
