Piotr Sankowski is a Polish computer scientist. He is a habilitated doctor of mathematical sciences and an associate professor at the Institute of Computer Science, Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics and Mechanics, University of Warsaw. His research interests focus on the practical application of algorithms across various fields, including economic applications, learning data structures, and parallel algorithms for data science.

He has also held leadership positions, serving as the president of the management board at IDEAS NCBR, a research and development center focused on artificial intelligence and the digital economy, from 2021 to 2024. Additionally, he is a co-founder of the spin-off company MIM Solutions.

Piotr Sankowski is notable for being the first Pole to have received four European Research Council (ERC) grants. He earned doctorates in computer science and physics from the University of Warsaw and the Polish Academy of Sciences, respectively.
