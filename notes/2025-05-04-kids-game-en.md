My Talking Angela 2+ is a virtual pet game developed by Outfit7 Limited, released on September 8, 2023, exclusively for Apple Arcade. It’s the sequel to My Talking Angela, part of the popular Talking Tom & Friends franchise, and offers a vibrant, kid-friendly experience with a PEGI 3 rating. Players care for Angela, a stylish cat living in a big city, engaging in activities like dancing, baking, martial arts, and jewelry making. The game features extensive customization options, allowing players to dress Angela in fashionable outfits, style her hair, and apply makeup. Mini-games like Donut Spin and puzzles test reflexes and problem-solving skills, while travel adventures let Angela explore new destinations. Players can also collect stickers to unlock rewards. With no ads or in-app purchases, My Talking Angela 2+ emphasizes creativity and fun, appealing to fans of fashion, cooking, and pet-care games.[](https://talking-tom-games.fandom.com/wiki/My_Talking_Angela_2)[](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/my-talking-angela-2/id6446493209)
