My Talking Angela 2+ is a virtual pet game developed by Outfit7 Limited, released on September 8, 2023, exclusively for Apple Arcade. It’s the sequel to My Talking Angela, part of the popular Talking Tom & Friends franchise, and offers a vibrant, kid-friendly experience with a PEGI 3 rating. Players care for Angela, a stylish cat living in a big city, engaging in activities like dancing, baking, martial arts, and jewelry making. The game features extensive customization options, allowing players to dress Angela in fashionable outfits, style her hair, and apply makeup. Mini-games like Donut Spin and puzzles test reflexes and problem-solving skills, while travel adventures let Angela explore new destinations. Players can also collect stickers to unlock rewards. With no ads or in-app purchases, My Talking Angela 2+ emphasizes creativity and fun, appealing to fans of fashion, cooking, and pet-care games.[](https://talking-tom-games.fandom.com/wiki/My_Talking_Angela_2)[](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/my-talking-angela-2/id6446493209)

**Cut the Rope 3** is a physics-based puzzle game developed by ZeptoLab, released exclusively for Apple Arcade on October 13, 2023. Unlike its predecessors, which focused on feeding candy to the adorable green monster Om Nom, this third installment introduces a new adventure where players guide Om Nom’s son, Nibble Nom, back to Om Nom through challenging levels. Featuring fully 3D graphics for the first time in the franchise, the game takes players on a vibrant journey across diverse locations like forests, caves, and pirate-themed maps, selected via Om Nom’s compass. 

The core gameplay retains the series’ signature rope-cutting mechanics but adds innovative elements like whips for swinging between platforms, balloons for floating, and other unique tools to solve puzzles. Players aim to collect up to 10 stars per level, with scoring based on stars gathered and attempts made, while navigating obstacles and using level objects strategically. The objective is to reunite Nibble Nom with Om Nom and discover new creatures called Nommies, enhancing the storyline. With no ads or in-app purchases, Cut the Rope 3 offers a family-friendly experience, blending nostalgic mechanics with fresh challenges for fans of physics-based puzzles and cute characters.[](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cut_the_Rope)[](https://www.cnet.com/tech/gaming/cut-the-rope-3-brings-a-new-adventure-to-apple-arcade/)[](https://www.imdb.com/es/title/tt35579405/)
