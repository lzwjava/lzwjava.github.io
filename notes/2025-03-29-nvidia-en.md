NVIDIA is currently working on several major technology areas:

1. CUDA: Still a cornerstone of NVIDIA's ecosystem, providing the programming model for GPU computing across scientific computing, AI, and data analytics.

2. Omniverse: Their platform for 3D workflows and virtual world simulations, enabling collaboration across different 3D applications.

3. AI and Deep Learning: 
   - Continued development of hardware (like their H100 and A100 GPUs)
   - Software frameworks like NVIDIA AI Enterprise
   - Domain-specific AI solutions for industries like healthcare, automotive, and manufacturing

4. Gaming and Graphics:
   - RTX technology and ray tracing capabilities
   - DLSS (Deep Learning Super Sampling)
   - GeForce NOW cloud gaming service

5. Automotive and Autonomous Systems:
   - DRIVE platform for autonomous vehicles
   - Metropolis platform for smart cities and infrastructure

6. High-Performance Computing:
   - HGX platform for data centers
   - Solutions for scientific research and simulation

7. Networking:
   - Mellanox networking technologies
   - DPUs (Data Processing Units)

8. Edge AI and Embedded Systems:
   - Jetson platform for robotics and edge applications
