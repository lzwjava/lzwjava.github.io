India is a vast country with numerous major cities that are important for their cultural, historical, economic, and political significance. Here are some of the **main cities in India**:

### **Metro Cities (Major Urban Centers)**  
1. **Mumbai** – Financial capital, home to Bollywood.  
2. **Delhi** (including **New Delhi**, the capital of India) – Political hub, historical sites.  
3. **Bangalore (Bengaluru)** – IT capital, tech hub.  
4. **Hyderabad** – IT & pharmaceutical industries, rich history.  
5. **Chennai** – Automobile & manufacturing hub, cultural center.  
6. **Kolkata** – Cultural & literary capital, major port city.  

### **Other Major Cities**  
7. **Pune** – Education & IT hub.  
8. **Ahmedabad** – Business & textile center.  
9. **Surat** – Diamond & textile industry.  
10. **Jaipur** – "Pink City," tourism & heritage.  
11. **Lucknow** – Capital of Uttar Pradesh, known for Nawabi culture.  
12. **Kanpur** – Industrial & leather hub.  
13. **Nagpur** – Geographical center of India, logistics hub.  
14. **Indore** – Cleanest city in India (as per Swachh Survekshan).  
15. **Thane** – Fast-growing suburb of Mumbai.  
16. **Bhopal** – Capital of Madhya Pradesh, lakes & history.  
17. **Visakhapatnam (Vizag)** – Port city & naval base.  
18. **Patna** – Capital of Bihar, historical significance.  
19. **Vadodara (Baroda)** – Cultural & educational hub.  
20. **Coimbatore** – Industrial city in Tamil Nadu.  

### **Emerging & Fast-Growing Cities**  
- **Gurgaon (Gurugram)** – Corporate & financial hub near Delhi.  
- **Noida** – IT & business hub in NCR.  
- **Ghaziabad** – Industrial city near Delhi.  
- **Chandigarh** – Planned city, capital of Punjab & Haryana.  
- **Kochi (Cochin)** – Major port city in Kerala.  
- **Bhubaneswar** – Smart city, capital of Odisha.  
- **Guwahati** – Largest city in Northeast India.
