## Sleeping Socks

*2025.06.24*

I have been using sleeping socks for months. They are great. I regret not thinking of this idea and trying it sooner.

Previously, I usually slept with the air conditioner on at around 23 or 24 degrees in the summer. I used a quilt to sleep as if it were winter in Guangzhou.

It costs a lot of electricity to run the air conditioner all night for months. In recent years, I have tried many new ideas. One day, I started to think about it.

Why did I need a quilt? It was because my feet would get cold without one. I could turn up the temperature of the air conditioner, but even at 27 or 28 degrees, my feet would still feel cold. If I turned the air conditioner up to 29 or 30 degrees, it would be pointless and better to shut it off. However, that would be too hot.

Then I tried finding these sleeping socks on Pinduoduo, and they were great.

Now, I set the air conditioner to 27 or 28 degrees and wear these sleeping socks all night. It works perfectly. 

But why don't I use a pair of normal socks? They are too tight and not comfortable.

## Bathe More Often To Embrace Sweating In The Summer

Nowadays, many people like to enjoy air conditioning instead of going outside in summer, myself included. However, sweating may actually be a good thing.

Why don't I like sweat? Because it makes clothes hard and uncomfortable to wear. But if I wash my body often, on weekends, I can let myself bathe two or three times a day.

Bathing just costs clothes and laundry. However, the benefit is that you are more willing to go outside and do some housework. That is totally a win. That makes us healthier.

How often should we sweat? There is no rigid schedule for that.

But the thing about bathing is that we often do it in our homes. If done outside, it is probably expensive, like when ordering a 3-hour hourly room in a hotel that costs around 100 CNY in big cities of China.

That's an area to improve. Probably, one day, there will be a 30-minute room option for people to bathe, costing 30 CNY each time. I think that's a user need.

Besides hotels, where can we bathe? There are swimming pools, commercial basketball playgrounds, and sports centers. It's good to go there to bathe.
