Recently, I moved my dishwasher from the home of my parents to my home in same city. The dishwasher is Media 13 Q7. I bought in 2019 summar when working in Beijing. 

And at the start of 2020, I used deppon delivery to help it move to Guangzhou.

I found a technican guy from AMap's home applicance repair service. I paid him for 750 CNY for helping us repairing and installing it in my home.

The story is a little long. We work from 3pm to 8pm, to finally make it work. In the middle, he went out to buy some material.

There are two or three times when we think it is ok. And then run a dishing program, but then after some time, the floor is all water.

The first time is that the exhale pipe of dishwasher has some hole. This is quick to observe and fix. However, it still leaks the water from the bottom.  We used some seats to make dishwasher high so we can know where to leak the water. It is like checking the car. 

We disassemble the dishwaer and check, and find that some inner pipe has some holes. The technican used the tape to pack it. 

Unfortunatly, there is still another place that has hole. We fixed again. 

Finally, around 8pm, it was good. This procedure reminds me of software development. In retrosepct, these issues are natural and reasonable. It is just out of our expectation but it follows the princinple of material and time.
