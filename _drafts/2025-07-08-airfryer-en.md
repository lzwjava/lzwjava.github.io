Air Fryer become my faviourite cooking tool recently. I bought Midea Air Fryer KZE5004 with around 126 CNY.

I made below dishes. The experience is really enjoying.

The dish at the last here is not cooking with air fryer, but just a pot to cook noddles. As it is my recent cooking dish, I place here too.

Whether you would like a cooking tool, the most plausiable judgement is how many dishes you would make with it.

Air fryer put my electric oven eating dusk. I actually want to use them but won't be allowed with my wife as she complains that will cost too much space and make our dining room and kitchen stuffed as there are already lots of tools and dinner plates.

This is reasonable as my wife will come to vist me at the weekend. I don't want to make things clustered. If I live alone always, I will move the electric oven back to dining room. 

Actually, they have some benifits. One is that you can use electric oven and air fryer at the same time, espcailly you have mutliple dishes to cook. Another is that sometimes you can compare the nuance difference using them with same ingredients. The third one is that we don't waste good product there to let them sit instead of working.

Always using one tool make one bored too. So I tried the traditional cooking with a pot to make noodles. I remember in 2020 and 2021, I made such dishes sometimes in my parents' home and sometimes made short videos about them.

At that time, it seems very easy to cook. But as now, I don't cook often and even sometimes find air fryer cooking a little trouble and want to order delivery food as busy at that moment. So I haven't tried to cook with frying pan or pot much. So today I am happy to do so. 

I bought packets of beef ball to prepare cooking them with air fryer and now I want to cook them with pot too.

The noddles is delicious. I used a lot of water to cover the ingredents and then scoop the water out. Then I will put flavor packet into pot. The final dish is great.
