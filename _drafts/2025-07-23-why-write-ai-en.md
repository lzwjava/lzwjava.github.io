The most important reason we still need write in AI era is it helps us think better. It is easy to use AI to help expand ideas or thoughts. But AI can't help you think.

I am not against that we can use AI to fix grammar. But like using prompt to make text more coherent is probably something that shows we don't think clearly.

Recently, I have some thoughts about AI-first architecture in modern development. I sent some thinking to my engineer friend. And I use that to expand an [essay](./notes/ai-first-architecture-evaluation-en).

Surely, AI help compelete my thoughts. It is good to read AI answer or evalutions of my points. But after one month, I think I will forget them.

Paul Graham wrote [The age of the essay](https://paulgraham.com/essay.html). He discuss something similar ideas.

Also, if you learn with AI and just read with AI answers, it has some problem when you take exams. You found that you are still not able to work out a lot of quiz.

Why? Because if you don't think out them by yourself, you can't know the stuff completely. Yin Wang in his essay, [Learning Wisdom](https://www.yinwang.org/blog-cn/2019/07/12/learning-philosophy) mentioned this also.

It seems the quiz in exams is similar to what you read in previous learning. Actually, you didn't comprehend much of that. So when you need to think out answers for yourself in the exam which forbid you using Internet, you are unable to think out.

But if AI can't help us write, then we will lose much of effect about them. What we need to use carefully about AI is that we need to think the important decision for ourselves.

AI can tell how we do or how we react in current situations. But AI couldn't know our heart. What we do and how we react is largely depend on who we are , what's mental state of us, and what's surrounding in us. 

AI can't know much of that context, so it can't help us how to act. It may points out the solutions to tell us to jump to growing startup or prepare exams. But it can't help us with actual steps. 

Possibly, the AI use is still limited. We need to use mobile phone or laptop to chat with AI chatbots. Now , we have AI glasses like meta quest. But they are still expensive and not available globablly.

But what if we can use AI anytime anywhere? Is it good that we use AI and follow them to act. It is hard. It is hard to follow exact steps to achieve something we thought we want.

Because it is probably that target is wrong. The target or goals we tell AI to help us achieve may be wrong, unrealistic, pusuring validation of others. So not to mention the exact steps.

We know what to do as we are grown up. That's shaped by a life of experience. It is about our journey, how we interact with sourroudings. In our mind, there are probably millions of thoughts. With that much of thoughts, then we decide what to do every day, every week, every year.

For something what to do , how to live a good life, how to make important decisions is that AI can't help us. We better write down our thoughts and reflect a lot on them.

We can think if we don't write down. So why we write down? Because like Paul Graham says, when you formed a sentence, you will have new thoughts. When we construct our thoughts, we are actaully thinking. And it triggers new thoughts. This kind of thoughts is not easily to have by pure thinking. 

It is like doing math. When you write steps down in the paper, you will probably have more chance to find answers. It is hard to do it by pure thinking.
