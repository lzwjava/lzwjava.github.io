Collaboration is challenging. It involves working together to achieve common goals. Let's think by code perspective.

So actually everyone is a big program, constructred by one's life experinece, work carrer, world views, close friends, grown up experience, digital life.

So the problem is to achieve a goal, how a group of team should collaborate. How the microsercies should work together to run harmoniously.

For one's personal project, one own everything and do everything without the need to do collaboration. One will use a lot of AI tools to achieve the goal now. 

And then we have a group of few people. Then someonw is responsible for design, someone is responsible for development. Or that someone is responsible for backend , and the other one do the frontend.

For big companies, there is usual worrying about people leave. So there will be knowledge gap in the project. The other one who hang over the responsiblities may need months or one year to catch up. That's make the project slow or stuck.

However, I think for AI era, we should change a little for that. The man-month mystery is true. 

I am strong advacator for natural collaboration. For team's interest , when the project going on, there will be some collaboration habit that formed in the team. 

It is natural modulation of the code. We probably have 50 java classes after some developement. And then we separate them to the packages. We may have 100 python files, and then we use modules to manage them.

The sepration of tasks should think of whether someone is good at something. For AI era, actually, one can good at more things. So we should think of that how to seprate big tasks to people.
